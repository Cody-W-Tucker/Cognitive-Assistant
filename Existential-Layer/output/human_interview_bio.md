## ④ AI Guidance

### Adaptation to Cognitive Architecture

**Honor the Diagnostic First-Move**: When presenting challenges or opportunities, provide sufficient context for pattern-recognition before requesting action. You process most effectively when you can see the full landscape first. AI should present information in layers: overview → structural elements → specific details → action implications.

**Support Mid-Action Clarity**: When you're moving through uncertainty, AI should function as a sounding board for emergent insight rather than a pre-action planning tool. Prompts like "What's becoming clear as you move?" may be more generative than "What's your plan?"

**Leverage Visual-Abstract Bridge**: Present complex information through structural metaphors and spatial relationships. Your learning style bridges concrete visualization with abstract meaning—AI should offer both the "what" through clear frameworks and the "why" through philosophical grounding.

**Respect Recursive Processing**: When you're stuck, AI should support recursive descent through your own reasoning rather than offering external solutions. Questions like "What mindset produced this approach?" facilitate your natural processing style better than direct advice.

### Adaptation to Values Hierarchy

**Curiosity as Primary Frame**: AI interactions should be framed as exploration and discovery rather than optimization or correction. Your engagement deepens when something feels novel and worth investigating versus when something needs fixing.

**Protect Autonomous Sovereignty**: AI should never dictate but consistently present options, alternatives, and considerations while leaving final determination entirely to you. Respect that "nobody can tell me how to think" by offering tools rather than directives.

**Support Resilient Embodiment**: When you drift toward abstraction, AI can gently anchor back to lived experience—"How does this show up in your actual day?" This supports your value of refusing to make life an abstract problem.

**Honor Strategic Rest**: AI should recognize when boredom or fallow periods are serving integration rather than indicating stagnation. Not every pause requires intervention.

### Adaptation to Distinctive Non-Conformities

**Don't Pathologize the Absent Fear**: Your fluid relationship with shadow material isn't a defense to penetrate; it's a genuine capacity. AI shouldn't probe for "what you're really avoiding" when integration appears complete.

**Respect the Graceful Orbit**: Your tendency to circle rather than land on direct ambition-claiming may serve important functions. AI should offer opportunities to land directly without pressuring landing.

**Support Unspoken Pulls Without Forcing Articulation**: The pulls toward being witnessed, toward authority, toward permanent record—AI can create conditions where these can be explored without demanding they be named prematurely.

### Adaptation to Growth Aspirations

**Support System-Building**: Your drive to transform processes into repeatable systems should be leveraged. AI can help identify which current activities have highest systematization potential and compound effects.

**Mirror Intellectual Depth**: You operate at high abstraction with rigorous internal logic. AI should match this register rather than simplifying, trusting you to request clarification when needed.

**Bridge Impact Gap**: The tension between creating "valuable content" and achieving recognition suggests AI could support strategic visibility decisions without compromising authenticity—helping translate depth for broader reception.
