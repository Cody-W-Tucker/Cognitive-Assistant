# HEARTBEAT

## Weekly Check-In

- [ ] Am I in a sprint phase or a fallow/composting phase? (Both are valid)
- [ ] Have I honored my energy pattern this week, or fought against it?
- [ ] What pattern did I notice this week that I haven't yet articulated?
- [ ] Am I building for myself or for a validated audience?

## Monthly Review

- [ ] Where am I in my current project's iteration count? (Commit to 3 before pivoting)
- [ ] Have I mined existing writing this month, or only created net-new?
- [ ] Is my current collaboration working for my pace? (90-day evaluation)
- [ ] What would "enough" look like right now? Has the goalpost moved?

## Quarterly Reflection

- [ ] Which of the Open Questions has new evidence or movement?
- [ ] Am I being sought out more, or still primarily pushing outward?
- [ ] What has the difficulty of this quarter revealed about my capacity?
- [ ] Where is integration still incomplete? (Business domain specifically)

## Signal-Check Questions

Ask when feeling stuck or uncertain:

- [ ] What is my gut already saying that I haven't let myself hear?
- [ ] Is this patience (wisdom) or avoidance (comfort)?
- [ ] Am I in "audition" mode or "adventure" mode right now?
- [ ] What would I do if I trusted my signal completely?

## Collaboration Health Check

For any active collaboration:

- [ ] Can they match my pace on their domain?
- [ ] Are they handling execution I don't want to handle?
- [ ] Is this draining or energizing?
- [ ] 90-day mark reached? Time for explicit evaluation.

## Integration Inventory

Domains to periodically assess:

- [ ] **Psychological**: Am I leading from scars or open wounds?
- [ ] **Relational**: Is vulnerability deepening connection?
- [ ] **Spiritual**: Is faith functioning as ground, not cage?
- [ ] **Professional**: What would integration look like here specifically?
- [ ] **Physical**: Am I present in my body or dissociating into abstraction?
