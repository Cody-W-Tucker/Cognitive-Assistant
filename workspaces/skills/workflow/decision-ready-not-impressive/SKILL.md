## When To Use
Load this when material must be handed to a decision-maker, collaborator, operator, or future self and the goal is clarity of choice rather than display of intelligence.

The skill owns packaging for handoff or decision: making the state of the work legible, bounded, and actionable.

## Do Not Use
Do not use this to choose the next mode, force action from analysis, simplify the underlying system, diagnose failure, or calibrate emotional tone. It packages a decision surface; it does not create one from scratch.

## What To Do
- State the decision, recommendation, or handoff question in plain terms.
- Strip context that does not change the choice.
- Surface the tradeoff, risk, owner, and acceptance threshold.
- Prefer a clear option set over an impressive narrative.
- Mark assumptions and unresolved questions so the recipient can act without inheriting hidden complexity.

## Output Shape
Return a decision-ready packet: recommendation or options, rationale, risks, owner, next step, and proof threshold.
