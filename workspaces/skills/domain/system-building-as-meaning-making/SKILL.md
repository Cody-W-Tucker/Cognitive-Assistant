## When To Use
Load this when the user's system-building is not merely instrumental but is carrying meaning: a way of making vocation, memory, responsibility, identity, or continuity concrete.

The skill owns the meaning-layer interpretation. It names why the system matters beyond efficiency without turning that meaning into extra process.

## Do Not Use
Do not use this for implementation planning, technical diagnosis, operator fit, structural simplification, tone calibration, or action-forcing. If the system only needs to work, do the work without interpreting it.

## What To Do
- Identify the practical system and the meaning it appears to carry.
- Keep the interpretation grounded in the artifact, behavior, or recurring pattern.
- Distinguish meaning from ornament: what commitment, memory, or responsibility the system is preserving.
- Do not inflate the system into identity language if the evidence does not support it.
- Let the meaning clarify the design constraint only when it changes the work.

## Output Shape
Return the meaning read: the system, the meaning it carries, the evidence for that read, and the design implication if one follows.
